About

Delivering Excellence in Frozen Foods

At Riviera Foods, we take pride in being a trusted wholesaler and distributor of premium frozen food products, catering to a wide range of clients across retail, foodservice, and industrial sectors. With an unwavering commitment to quality and safety, we source only the finest products from leading suppliers to ensure our customers receive the best in frozen food solutions. We offer a diverse selection of frozen foods, including snacks, meats, and ready-to-cook delights, all packed to retain maximum freshness and convenience. Our product range is carefully curated to meet the varying needs of our clients, whether you are in the retail business or the foodservice industry. We are proud to offer products from renowned brands like:
✔ SFP Foodstuffs Pvt. Ltd.
✔ Belchick
✔ Godrej Tyson Foods Ltd.
✔ Godrej Yummies
These trusted brands, alongside our own premium offerings, ensure we deliver top-tier quality to every customer, every time. Our products undergo stringent quality checks to guarantee superior taste, texture, and food safety. Specializing in seamless bulk supply, we ensure that our clients have consistent access to high-quality frozen food products throughout the year. Whether you're looking for frozen appetizers, burger patties, or premium meat cuts, Riviera Foods has you covered. With our state-of-the-art storage and logistics network, we prioritize timely delivery, ensuring your products reach you in perfect condition while maintaining their freshness. From order to delivery, we provide a hassle-free experience, allowing you to focus on your business with confidence. Riviera Foods – your trusted partner for the best in frozen food solutions.

Mission Statement

To deliver high-quality, nutritious frozen foods with a commitment to innovation, sustainability, and excellence. We ensure freshness, convenience, and ethical sourcing in every product.

Vision Statement

To be a globally trusted frozen food brand, known for outstanding quality, eco-friendly practices, and accessible, wholesome food for all.

Company Philosophy

We prioritize quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction while fostering discipline, empathy, and sustainable growth.
